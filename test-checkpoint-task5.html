Test Summary

Manual Test Instructions

  1. The camera should initialize automatically when the component loads
  2. You should see a live video preview with a frame overlay guide
  3. Click the "Capturer" button to take a photo
  4. The photo should be captured, compressed to 300x400px JPEG, and displayed
  5. You should see "Reprendre" and "Valider" buttons
  6. Click "Reprendre" to discard the photo and restart the camera
  7. Capture another photo and click "Valider" to confirm
  8. The camera should stop and the photo data should be logged
